Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-01

SharkTeethFinder (“the App”) shows non-personalized ads and offers an auto-renewable subscription. We do not collect names, email addresses, or precise location. The App does not access the IDFA and does not show an App Tracking Transparency prompt.

What We Collect

  • On device only: subscription status and expiration time (cached for offline use), rewarded-ad time counters.
  • Advertising service data: Google AdMob may process device/usage data (e.g., IP address, coarse device info, app interactions) to deliver and limit ads, detect fraud, and measure performance.

Ads (Google AdMob)

We force non-personalized ads (NPA=1). Google is the processor of advertising data under its own policies: Google Privacy & Terms / Partners.

Purchases

Subscriptions are processed by Apple’s StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment details. You can manage or cancel your subscription in iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

Do We “Track” Users?

No. The App does not link collected data with third-party data for cross-app tracking, does not access the IDFA, and serves only non-personalized ads.

Data Retention & Deletion

Local subscription cache and rewarded counters remain on your device and are removed when you delete the App. We do not keep server copies.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13.

User Choices

Because we do not store personal data on our servers, requests to access or delete personal data generally do not apply. For ad preferences, use Google’s controls: My Ad Center.
