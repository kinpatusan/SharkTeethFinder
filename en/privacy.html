Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-03-10

This app ("SharkTeeth Finder") displays non-personalized ads and offers auto-renewable subscriptions. We do not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or precise location data. This app does not access IDFA and does not display the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) dialog.

Information We Collect

  • Stored locally only: Subscription status and expiration date (for offline caching), reward ad remaining time counter, favorite tide locations, and collection photos/videos.
  • Location data: When you grant permission, approximate location is used to find nearby tide stations and save discovery locations. This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.
  • Ad-related data: Google AdMob may process IP addresses, device information, and in-app ad interactions for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and measurement.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app enforces non-personalized ads (NPA=1). Ad data handling follows Google's policies: Google Privacy Policy / Partner Information.

Purchases (Subscriptions)

Payments are processed by Apple's StoreKit, and we do not receive or store payment information. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

Tracking

We do not perform cross-app tracking by linking with third-party data. We do not collect IDFA, and ads are non-personalized.

Data Retention & Deletion

The local data mentioned above (subscription cache, reward time, favorite locations, collection data) is stored only on your device. This data is deleted when you uninstall the app. No server-side storage is performed.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). Since we do not collect any personal information, this app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). If children under 13 use this app, we recommend parental supervision.

Your Choices

Since we do not store personal data on our servers, there is generally nothing to disclose or delete upon request. For ad-related settings, please use Google's control panel: My Ad Center.
